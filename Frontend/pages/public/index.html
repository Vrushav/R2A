Rural Healthcare Access

Connecting Rural India to Better Healthcare

🌾Rural Healthcare Platform

R2A is a rural-first healthcare platform that connects patients, hospitals, doctors, ASHA workers and government healthcare services through one secure and easy-to-use digital ecosystem.

🏥 Government Healthcare

Connect with PHCs, CHCs and district hospitals through one unified platform.

🌾Rural First

Designed for villages with multilingual support, ASHA integration and offline accessibility.

🤖 AI Assisted Guidance

Receive intelligent department recommendations before booking an appointment.

How it works

1

👤 Register

Create your patient profile with your basic information to begin using R2A.

2

🤖 Get Guidance

Use the AI Assistant or select a department to find the right healthcare service.

3

📅 Book Appointment

Choose your village, hospital, doctor and available appointment slot.

4

🏥 Receive Care

Visit the hospital with your QR appointment pass and manage your healthcare journey digitally.
