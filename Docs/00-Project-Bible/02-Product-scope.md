# R2A – Product Scope

Version: 1.0

Status: Approved

---

# Purpose

R2A is a Rural Healthcare Accessibility Platform designed to reduce the barriers between rural communities and healthcare services through a simple, multilingual, and scalable digital ecosystem.

The platform focuses on improving healthcare accessibility rather than replacing existing hospital management systems.

---

# Target Users

Primary Users

- Rural Patients
- Family Members
- ASHA Workers

Secondary Users

- Doctors
- Hospital Staff
- Hospital Administrators

Future Users

- Government Healthcare Departments
- NGOs
- Public Health Organizations

# What R2A Does NOT Include

The following are outside the scope of Version 1:

- Online Payment Gateway
- Electronic Medical Records (Full EMR)
- Laboratory Management
- Billing System
- Insurance Claim Processing
- Pharmacy Inventory Management
- Wearable Device Integration

These may be considered in future versions after the core platform is stable.

---

# Success Criteria

Version 1 of R2A will be considered successful when:

- Patients can register and manage their profiles.
- Appointments can be booked digitally.
- Doctors can manage appointments.
- Hospital administrators can manage doctors and departments.
- The platform provides a simple and accessible experience for rural communities.
